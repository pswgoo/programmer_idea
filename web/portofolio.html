Arsenal Football Club is a professional football club based in Highbury, London, that plays in the Premier League, the top flight of English football. The club has won 13 League titles, 12 FA Cups (a joint record), two League Cups, 14 FA Community Shields, one UEFA Cup Winners' Cup and one Inter-Cities Fairs Cup.

1970 FAIRS CUP FINAL

After 17 years without a trophy, the glory days returned to Highbury with a dramatic victory over Belgian side Anderlecht in the 1970 Fairs Cup Final.
